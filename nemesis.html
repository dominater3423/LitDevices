  • Nemesis

Definition

A punishment or defeat that is deserved and cannot be avoided.

Example

In Oedipus Rex, by Sophocles, the king tries to defy his fate. He kills his father and impregnates his mother, out of ignorance. His attempt to defy fate, that fate is his punishment.
