Definition

A humorous play in which the characters become involved in unlikely situations, or the humor in this type of play.

Example

In Oliver Goldsmith's play, She Stoops to Conquer, he pokes fun of the manners of a particular society. It goes beyond that as well, and even pokes fun of the very plot of the play itself.
