Project Instructions

We are studying literary devices in English class. For this we were given an assignment where we had to create some sort of media, for me it's a website, and explain/gives examples of 5-10 literary devices. There will be a page dedicated to each term, and on these pages we need to hit three requirements:

  1. definition of literary device
  2. example and iteration of device in context
  3. image

The outcomes which have to be met are literary/rhetorical terms and support/evidence. The advanced descriptions are down below.

Literary & Rhetorical Terms

I can demonstrate my understanding of literary terms, including figurative and rhetorical devices, by answering comprehension and analysis questions with above 80% accuracy. I can incorporate well explained analysis of figurative and rhetorical devices in my writing, providing close examination of figurative language and illustrating how an author utilizes those devices to create meaning.

Support/Evidence

I can choose the most relevant and important details, descriptions, and/or evidence to add depth or complexity to my ideas in support of my purpose. I can integrate quotes, using only the key language from the source text. I can demonstrate the ability to summarize, paraphrase, or provide direct quotes when appropriate, utilizing the best approach to incorporate supporting evidence and information.
